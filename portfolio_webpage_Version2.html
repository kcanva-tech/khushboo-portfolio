About Me

Hello! I'm Khushboo Singh, a passionate graphic designer with a keen eye for detail and a love for creating visually stunning designs that captivate and communicate.

I specialize in digital design across multiple platforms, bringing creativity and technical expertise to every project. My work spans from food catalogs and product designs to magazine covers and social media assets.

With proficiency in Photoshop, Illustrator, and Canva, I blend technical skill with artistic vision to deliver designs that not only look amazing but also effectively communicate your message.
